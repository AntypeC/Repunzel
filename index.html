About Us

Based in Australia, right next to Redfern Iglu student accommodation. Budget friendly for students, offers selective student discount based on the major of the student's studies. Here at Repunzel, we offer a range of options, including hair trims, hair coloring, hair washing and blow dry, hair straightening, beard trimming and grooming, hair treatments, oil massages, scalp massage, head spa, selling shampoos.
